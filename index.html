Popular Samosa Types

🥔 Aloo Samosa

Traditional potato-filled samosa with Indian spices and crispy outer layer.

🧀 Paneer Samosa

Delicious paneer stuffing mixed with masala and herbs for rich flavor.

🌽 Corn Samosa

Sweet corn mixed with cheese and spices, loved by modern food lovers.

🍫 Chocolate Samosa

A sweet twist with melted chocolate inside crispy samosa sheets.

Benefits & Ingredients

🌿 Healthy Spices

Indian spices like cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala improve taste and digestion.

🥔 Energy Source

Potatoes provide carbohydrates and energy for daily activities.

🧅 Fresh Vegetables

Onion, peas, coriander, and green chili add freshness and nutrition.

🔥 Crispy Taste

Perfect crunchy texture makes samosa one of the most loved snacks in India.
